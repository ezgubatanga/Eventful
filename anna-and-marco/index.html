Our Story

A Roman beginning

Anna and Marco met on a rainy Tuesday at a bookshop near the Pantheon — both reaching for the last copy of the same novel. Marco insisted she take it. Anna insisted he read it first. They compromised over espresso, and neither wanted the conversation to end.

What followed were long walks along the Tiber, Sunday markets, and evenings that stretched past midnight. Two years of quiet mornings and loud laughter.

Marco proposed under the stars at Villa Borghese, the city glittering below them. Anna said yes before he finished the question.

Explore Rome

Things to do nearby

A few of our favourite Roman experiences — whether you're arriving early or staying on after the wedding.

History

Colosseum & Roman Forum

Walk through 2,000 years of history at Rome's most iconic landmark. Book timed entry tickets in advance to avoid queues.

Art & Culture

Vatican Museums & Sistine Chapel

Home to Michelangelo's masterpiece ceiling. Pre-book a timed entry or early-morning access tour for the best experience.

Nature & Art

Villa Borghese & Gallery

Stroll the park where Marco proposed, then visit the Borghese Gallery for Bernini sculptures and Caravaggio canvases. Timed entry required.

Local Life

Trastevere Neighborhood Walk

Wander the cobblestoned streets of the ceremony neighbourhood — golden-lit trattorias, ivy-draped piazzas, and the finest gelato in Rome.

Food & Market

Campo de' Fiori Morning Market

Rome's liveliest daily market runs until midday. Artisan cheeses, seasonal produce, and fresh flowers — followed by espresso at a nearby bar.

Iconic

Trevi Fountain at Sunrise

Arrive just after dawn to experience the Trevi without the crowds. Toss a coin, make a wish, and catch the soft morning light on Bernini's baroque masterpiece.
