Our Story

How it all began

Sofia and Carlos first crossed paths at a photography workshop in Vigan — both chasing the same golden-hour shot of the cobblestone streets. Carlos accidentally knocked over Sofia's tripod, and the rest, as they say, is history.

What started with a shared laugh over a broken tripod turned into weekend road trips, late-night conversations, and two hearts that couldn't imagine separate futures.

Three years, twelve provinces, and one very dramatic proposal at the top of Mayon Volcano later — they're finally making it official. And they couldn't be more ready.

Only if your invitation indicates a plus-one. Kindly reach out to us directly if you have questions.

Is parking available?

Yes — both the parish and Wolfgang's Steakhouse have on-site parking. Valet is available at the reception.

Are children welcome?

Children are welcome at the ceremony. The reception is adults-only (18+).

What time should I arrive?

Please arrive at least 30 minutes before the ceremony. The church doors open at 2:30 PM.
